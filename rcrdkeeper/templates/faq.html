FAQ Frequently Asked Questions

What is rcrdkeepr?
Rcrdkeepr is a vinyl record management system, used to keep track and organize your records for simple cataloging and information retrevial. By being powered by Rdio you are able to get information automatically including artwork, release date, album length, etc.
Can I access album information?
If you double click an album cover you will be able to view basic album information.
Why does my album come up with no information?
Being powered by Rdio means being limited to information only available in the Rdio catalog (which still includes 18 million+ songs).
Can I add my own infomation to an album?
Yes, whether you want to add it because Rdio does not have it available or you want to do customization all records are editable. By clicking the pencil above the record you get a pop-up of options that can be modified.
I would like to make a suggestion.
Great! All suggestions to improve the user experience are welcome just select 'Contact' from above and for issue type select Suggestion and fill out what it is you would like and it will be taken into consideration.
I have encountered an error.
Please submit any errors by going to the 'Contact' form and selecting 'Error' as type and please describe the error and we will get it fixed.
I have forgotten my password.
On the login page there is a forgot password option using that you will be able to reset your password. If a problem persist with your account please contact us and we will resolve it immediately.
